Sugar Cookie Recipe!

Ingredients List:

  1. Flour
  2. Leaveners
  3. Butter
  4. Sugar
  5. Egg

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees - Temperature will be done in Farenheight for this recipe.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Mix all wet ingredients in a different bowl - Make sure to mix dry and wet seperately!
  4. Slowly pour the flour mixture into the butter mixture.
  5. Roll the dough into balls.
  6. Place dough balls onto the baking sheet.
  7. Bake cookies until golden brown on the edges.
