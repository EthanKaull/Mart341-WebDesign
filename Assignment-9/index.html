How To Construct A Paper Airplane

Materials

  • 1 Piece of Printer Paper
      (8 Inches by 10 Inches)

Step 1

Start by folding the paper width wise, and unfolding it.

Step 2

Fold the top corners to the center fold line.

Step 3

Fold the tip down to meet the edge of step two's folds.

Step 4

Fold the left and right side into the original fold line from step one.

Step 5: Finishing the plane

Fold back on the original fold line from step one. Fold down your wings and give it a test flight!
